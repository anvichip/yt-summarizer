The iPhone 15 series offers several impressive features. The phones now come with USB Type-C, offering more versatility in charging and connectivity. The design features rounded edges and a premium matte finish. The display has smaller bezels and a dynamic island, replacing the traditional notch. The camera has been upgraded with a 48-megapixel main sensor and improved HDR capabilities. The performance is powered by the A16 Bionic chip, providing flagship-level speed and efficiency. The battery life is good, especially on the iPhone 15 Plus model. The phones offer a variety of color options and start at Rs. 79,900 for the iPhone 15 and Rs. 89,900 for the iPhone 15 Plus. Overall, the iPhone 15 series provides incremental upgrades compared to previous models, giving users the option to decide if these changes are significant enough to justify an upgrade.
