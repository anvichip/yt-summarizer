The review is about the new Apple iPhone 15 and iPhone 15 Plus. The phones come in pink, green, yellow, blue, and black colors and have a premium matte finish. They have an aluminum frame and ceramic shields. The phones have a dynamic island and physical features such as side buttons, volume buttons, SIM tray, USB-C port, microphone, speaker grills, and a dual camera setup (48MP main camera and 12MP ultra-wide camera). The screen is a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display with 2000 nits peak brightness. The camera has features such as portrait mode with automatic depth capture, 4K 60fps video, and cinematic mode. The phones are powered by the Apple A16 Bionic chip, which offers flagship performance. They have features like stereo speakers, ultra-wideband chip, navigational system, USB-C port, and compatibility with Apple Watch and AirPods. The battery life is good and the phones run on iOS 17. The price starts at Rs. 79,900 for iPhone 15 and Rs. 89,900 for iPhone 15 Plus. The phones are available in different colors and will be available by September. The reviewer finds the phones packed with features and likes the new rounded edges and matte finish.
